About Us

Empowering Creative Expression, One Garment at a Time

Frill is a Pakistan-based B2C e-commerce platform that empowers customers to design and order fully customized printed clothing — including T-shirts, hoodies, polo shirts, and tote bags — entirely online.

Shoppers can upload their own artwork, use Frill's built-in web-based design studio to create original prints, or choose from a curated template library. Orders are then fulfilled through Frill's in-house manufacturing facility using industry-leading Direct-to-Garment (DTG) and sublimation printing technology.

Every product is crafted to guarantee vibrant, wash-resistant, premium-quality results delivered straight to your doorstep across Pakistan.
