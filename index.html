Our Story

Born in a dorm room. Built in Pakistan. Worn everywhere.

🌱 Where It All Started

Frill was born out of a simple frustration: custom clothing in Pakistan was either outrageously expensive, poor quality, or required a minimum of 100 pieces. As university students, we wanted to print 5 hoodies for our club event — and couldn't find a single local platform that made it possible. So we built one.

🏭 Why We Print In-House

Every global print-on-demand platform outsources to third parties — which means longer delivery times, inconsistent quality, and zero control. At Frill, we invested in our own DTG and sublimation printers. That means every order that comes in goes directly to our production floor. Same-day printing. Colour-accurate output. Wash-resistant inks. This is the Frill difference.

🇵🇰 Proudly Pakistani

Pakistan's textile sector exports $16 billion annually — yet Pakistani consumers have had no access to a world-class custom clothing platform. Frill changes that. We source premium blank garments from local mills, print locally, and deliver locally. Every rupee stays in the ecosystem.

🎯 Where We're Going

We're not just building a print shop. Frill is the infrastructure for self-expression in South Asia — a platform where artists sell their designs, creators launch their merch, and brands customise their identity. The studio, the store, the manufacturing — it all points toward one vision: making creative self-expression accessible to everyone in Pakistan.

FAQ

Common questions

What's the minimum order quantity?
Just 1 piece! Frill accepts single-piece orders at no extra charge. This is one of our core USPs — no one else in Pakistan offers this with in-house production.
How long does delivery take?
3–5 business days nationwide. Islamabad orders can arrive in as little as 2 days. You'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email once dispatched.
What print methods do you use?
We use Direct-to-Garment (DTG) for cotton garments and dye-sublimation for polyester. Both methods produce vibrant, wash-resistant prints that last hundreds of washes.
Can I upload my own artwork?
Absolutely. Upload PNG, SVG, or JPEG files through the design studio. We recommend 300 DPI or higher for the sharpest print output. Our system processes your file automatically.
Do you accept Cash on Delivery?
Yes! We accept COD nationwide, plus JazzCash, Easypaisa, bank transfer, and all major credit/debit cards through our secure payment gateway.
