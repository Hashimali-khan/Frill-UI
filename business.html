Business Strategy

The Frill
Business Plan

A vertically integrated e-commerce model built for Pakistan's growing youth-driven fashion market.

Business Model

E-Tailer: Vertically Integrated

Frill designs, manufactures, and sells its own custom printed products directly to end consumers — owning the entire supply chain from digital design tool through physical production to last-mile delivery.

Primary Model
B2C – Business to Consumer

Frill manufactures and ships custom printed clothing directly to individual end consumers through its online platform at frill.pk. Full quality control, 40–55% gross margins on individual orders.

Secondary Model
B2B – Corporate & Bulk Orders

Bulk corporate orders for uniforms, event merchandise, and brand merch for SMEs. Tiered discounts for 20+ pieces with dedicated account manager and invoiced payment.

Revenue Streams

How Frill Makes Money

Revenue Stream Mechanism Est. Share Contribution
Direct Product Sales Customer pays per garment: base cost + print complexity surcharge + delivery fee 65%
Bulk / Corporate Orders Tiered discounts for 20+ pieces; dedicated account manager; invoiced payment 20%
Premium Design Templates Curated artist-designed templates sold at PKR 199–499 per licence 8%
White-Label Fulfilment Third-party brands send designs; Frill prints & ships under their branding (B2B2C) 7%
